00 · Dividers · Cards · Tabs — structural set v2

Rebuilt to spec. Dividers are now chamfered slab silhouettes — a horizontal bar with a descending foot, painted in the accent colour. Cards ship with generous padding (1.4rem horizontal) on the outer frame and inside each header/body/footer slot. Tabs use an interlocking peg-and-notch geometry: each tab's right edge sticks a peg out, the next tab's left edge bites a notch in, with a 4px flex gap between — irregular negative space reads as the connector. Active tabs fill solid with the accent and switch the label to bold uppercase BLACK so it punches through the fill.
